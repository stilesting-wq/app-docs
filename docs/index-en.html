Swiftfeed

Privacy-first AI RSS reader & personal knowledge workspace

Swiftfeed is an all-in-one tool that combines RSS feed reading, AI-assisted content creation, task management and cross-platform publishing. All your data is encrypted and stored locally on your device, with zero remote collection and tracking.

100% local storage · No analytics SDKs · You own all your data

Core Features

Multi-source RSS Reader

Subscribe to standard RSS & Atom feeds from blogs, news sites and independent creators. Import and export subscriptions via OPML for seamless migration and backup.

Custom AI Assistant

Connect your own AI API including OpenAI, Anthropic and Bailian. Generate article summaries, daily digests, writing drafts and creative ideas with full control over your AI provider.

Task & Habit Management

Organize todos, subtasks and daily check-ins with local notifications. Set auto-clean cycles from 7 to 180 days to automatically clear old content and save storage.

One-Click Social Publishing

Draft content inside the app and share directly to X, Threads and Instagram. All login credentials are encrypted locally and never sent to developer servers.

Multi-language TTS & Interface

Listen to articles hands-free with auto-switching text-to-speech. Native UI available in English, Simplified Chinese, Japanese and French.

Built for Privacy

  • No user data, device IDs or reading records are uploaded to any server
  • No integrated analytics, crash tracking or ad tracking SDKs
  • All data stored inside the iOS sandbox with system-level encryption
  • One-tap full data wipe; uninstalling permanently erases all local data
