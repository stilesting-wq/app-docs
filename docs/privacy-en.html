Swiftfeed Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Swiftfeed ("the App") is developed and operated by an individual developer. We take user privacy extremely seriously. This privacy policy clearly and transparently explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

1. Data Collection

Swiftfeed does not collect, upload, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) from users. The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising tracking SDKs. No user behavior data is collected or shared in the background.

2. Locally Stored Data

All data generated and used by the App is stored locally on your device only. The developer has no access to any of your data. Local data includes:

  • RSS feed configurations and fetched article content
  • AI chat history and conversation messages
  • Task management data, including to-do items, check-in records, and timelines
  • Saved articles and custom categories
  • App preferences and settings (display language, data retention period, etc.)
  • Post records and local drafts

All aforementioned data remains solely on your device. You may delete all local app data at any time within the App settings.

3. Third-Party Services & Data Transmission

Data will only be transmitted to third-party services when you actively initiate and authorize the corresponding features. No data is transmitted automatically in the background.

3.1 AI Chat
Your chat content will be sent to the AI service provider you manually configure for processing. The App integrates Alibaba Cloud Bailian API by default. You may also manually connect third-party providers including OpenAI and Anthropic. All third-party service usage is subject to the respective provider’s official privacy policy.

3.2 RSS Subscription
The App retrieves subscribed content via standard RSS/Atom protocols. Certain social media feeds are fetched through your self-hosted RSSHub instance. During standard network communication, your device IP address may be logged by the target server, which is a normal Internet communication behavior beyond our control.

3.3 Content Publishing
When you publish content to social platforms such as X (Twitter), data is transmitted through the official platform API. If you authorize your X account, the OAuth token is stored only on your local device and never uploaded to any server.

3.4 Remote Configuration
On startup, the App fetches a lightweight JSON configuration file from the developer’s server to update default feed recommendations and app-level configurations. This request carries no personal user data, device identifiers, or private information.

4. Device Permissions

All permissions requested by the App are strictly for functional purposes and will not be used for data collection or tracking:

  • Microphone: Used exclusively for voice input. Voice recognition is processed locally via Apple’s Speech framework. No voice data is uploaded externally.
  • Notifications: Used for local task reminders and daily digest alerts. All notifications are processed on-device without third-party push servers.
  • Photo Library: Used for selecting images in AI conversations via Apple’s PhotosPicker. The App only accesses the images you explicitly select.

5. On-Device Speech Recognition

The voice input feature utilizes Apple’s native Speech framework. All voice data is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to any external server. Apple may receive anonymized voice fragments during recognition processing, governed by Apple’s official privacy policy.

6. Data Security

  • All local data is encrypted via system-level sandbox encryption on iOS devices.
  • The developer has no ability to remotely access, read, modify, or retrieve your local data.
  • We will never sell, rent, share, or transfer your user data to any third parties for commercial or non-commercial purposes.
  • All app data will be permanently cleared once you uninstall the application from your device.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from any users, including children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through our App, please contact us promptly so we can handle relevant inquiries.

8. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy periodically. Revised terms will be posted within the App. Material changes will be notified via in-app announcements or update logs. Your continued use of the App after policy updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
