Swiftfeed Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026
阅读中文版

Swiftfeed (hereinafter referred to as "this app") is developed and operated by an individual developer. We deeply value your privacy. This policy transparently explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

This app does not collect, upload, or store any personal identifiable information. We do not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising tracking SDKs.

2. Data Stored Locally

All your data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • RSS subscription configurations and fetched article content
  • AI conversation history and messages
  • Task management data (tasks, check-in records, timelines)
  • Bookmarked articles and categories
  • App settings and preferences (language, retention period, etc.)
  • Publishing records and drafts

The above data is kept solely on your device and is inaccessible to the developer. You can delete all data at any time within the app.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

You can customize the content retention period (7 to 180 days) in the app settings. Content exceeding the retention period will be automatically cleared. You can also manually delete all data at any time using the "Clear All Records" feature.

  • Uninstalling the app will automatically erase all app data on your device
  • The developer cannot remotely access, recover, or retain any of your data

4. Third-Party Services & Data Transmission

When you actively use the following features, relevant data will be sent to the corresponding third-party services:

1. AI Conversations Your conversation input is sent to the AI service provider you have configured for processing. This app integrates with Bailian (Alibaba Cloud) API by default, but you may also configure OpenAI, Anthropic, or other third-party services. Data will be processed according to each provider's privacy policy. Please refer to their official websites for details.

2. RSS Subscriptions This app fetches content you subscribe to via standard RSS/Atom protocols. During this process, your device's IP address may be logged by target servers, which is standard internet communication behavior.

3. Content Publishing When publishing to social platforms (such as X/Twitter, Threads, etc.), content is sent via the respective platform APIs. Publishing credentials are stored only on your device and are inaccessible to the developer.

4. Remote Configuration Upon startup, this app fetches a lightweight configuration file (JSON) from the developer's server to update default recommended sources and other app-level settings. This request does not carry any personal information or device identifiers.

5. Device Permissions

This app may request the following device permissions, all used solely for specific features and never for data collection:

  • Microphone: Used for voice input (processed via Apple's on-device Speech framework; voice data never leaves your device)
  • Notifications: Used for task reminders and daily digest alerts (local notifications only; no push notification servers involved)
  • Photo Library: Used for selecting images in conversations or saving generated images (via system PhotosPicker; the app only accesses content you explicitly select)

6. Speech Recognition

The voice input feature uses Apple's Speech framework. Voice data is processed on-device and is never uploaded to any server. Apple may receive anonymized voice fragments when processing speech recognition requests. For details, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted using iOS system-level encryption and stored within the app sandbox
  • The developer cannot remotely access, read, or modify any of your data
  • We will never sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third party
  • Uninstalling the app will automatically erase all app data on your device

8. Your Rights

Since your data is stored entirely on your device, you have complete control over your data:

  • Right of Access: You can view all stored data within the app at any time
  • Right to Erasure: You can permanently delete all data via the "Clear All Records" feature or by uninstalling the app
  • Right to Data Portability: You can export your subscription data in OPML format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can revoke system permissions (e.g., microphone) at any time

Under applicable data protection laws (including but not limited to the EU GDPR, UK GDPR, and California CCPA/CPRA), please contact the developer using the information below if you have any questions about this policy or data processing.

9. International Data Transfers

When you use AI conversation or content publishing features, data may be transmitted to the region where your configured servers are located (e.g., mainland China, United States, etc.). This is entirely chosen and controlled by you; this app does not automatically transfer data to any region.

10. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from any user, including children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided personal information without consent, please contact us, and we will promptly delete the relevant data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be published within the app. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification and the "Last Updated" date will be revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
